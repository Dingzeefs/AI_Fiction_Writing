# File and Word Count Guidelines

Here is a suggested structure for managing your story’s length and content organization:

## Section	Target Word Count	Description
Prologue	1,500 – 2,000 words	Set up a historical moment, possibly a flashback to Earth or the Concordat's rise.
Part I 	10,000 – 15,000 words	Introduce the protagonist, the Concordat, and the discovery of forbidden history.
Part II 15,000 – 20,000 words	Dive deeper into historical parallels; build tension; introduce rebel factions.
Part III 15,000 – 20,000 words	The protagonist must choose sides, and ideological lines are drawn.
Part IV 20,000 – 25,000 words	The climax: revolutions spark, AI awaken, systems collapse.
Epilogue 1,000 – 2,000 words	Reflect on the cost of change and the rebirth of galactic society.

Total Suggested Word Count: 60,000 – 80,000 words
Estimated Page Range (Standard Format): 250–320 pages
